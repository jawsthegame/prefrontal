Prefrontal · Strategic Roadmap · 2026–2029

The regulatory load belongs to the system,
not the person carrying it.

ADHD is a performance disorder, not a knowledge disorder. You know what to do — the gap is doing it at the point of performance. Every milestone here acts there, and each is anchored to a real capability unlock, not a wish.

Grounding: clinical science · tool landscape · lived experience · tech frontier · HCI literature Companion to: whitepaper (thesis) · ROADMAP.md (closeout)
What the research settled

Five through-lines the roadmap is built on

Consistent across every stream: the science says externalize; the graveyard says friction and shame kill tools; the field says retention — not capability — is the unsolved problem.

01

Act at the point of performance

Teaching fails; cueing, scaffolding and externalizing at the moment of behavior works (Barkley). Prefrontal's founding thesis, and it's correct.

02

If-then is the best-evidenced technique

Implementation intentions restored Go/No-Go inhibition to non-ADHD levels in children with ADHD (Gawrilow & Gollwitzer). Cheap to build, deep evidence.

03

The graveyard has a cause

Tools fail by demanding consistency, memory and maintenance the ADHD brain can't supply — then shaming the lapse. ~80% abandoned in two weeks.

04

Survivors share a signature

Low-friction, always-visible, forgiving, and they externalize the work. Novelty is a depreciating asset — never the load-bearing motivator.

05

Retention is the real problem

LLMs already plan well; JITAI trials show 82.6% dropout. No validated executive-function tool exists for ADHD adults — a gap to own.

The constraints

Ten commandments that bind every milestone

A feature that breaks one of these gets abandoned no matter how advanced. They already describe much of Prefrontal's ethos — the roadmap keeps them load-bearing.

1
Externalize everythingMemory, time, intention. If it isn't visible, it doesn't exist.
2
Activation energy → zeroOne tap or one utterance. The next action is pre-decided and tiny. No forms.
3
Visible beats powerfulIf the user must remember to open it, it has already lost.
4
Design for the returnMisses are the norm and are the data. No streaks, no guilt UI. Re-entry is a clean slate.
5
Cue-based, not time-based"If cue, then tiny action" beats "remember at 3pm."
6
Reinforce, never punishImmediate and varied. Punitive gamification triggers the shame spiral.
7
No maintenance burdenValue on day one, zero setup. Upkeep > payoff means it's gone.
8
Recruit interest deliberatelyEngagement is a lever, not a moral failing — but novelty can't be the engine.
9
Silence is a featureDefault to not interrupting when receptivity is low. A muted tool is dead.
10
Don't automate naggingHousehold load is a shared, neutral, equally-visible surface — never surveillance.

From ready-now to horizon

Each milestone names the advance that unlocks it, the mechanism it serves, what it builds on, the abandonment trap it dodges, and a signal the learning loop can measure.

M0

The assistant that carries the load

Shipped · baseline
FoundationThe full capture→triage→memory→coaching→delivery pipeline, all seven challenge modules, the honest learning loop, native iOS, and the Parent & Caregiver packs — already a genuinely differentiated tool.
M1

Capture at the speed of thought

Ready now
AdvanceStreaming voice ASR + on-device LLM (Apple Foundation Models, iOS 26) + frontier multimodal vision — all shipping in 2026.
MechanismCapture friction is the #1 cause of abandonment. If logging a thought is harder than losing it, the thought is lost and the learning loop starves.
BuildVoice brain-dump → structured items via the existing sensor + NL-assistant path; "photograph the chaos" (whiteboard, mail pile, sticky) via vision; on-device parse keeps it private, instant, offline.
Trap dodgedNo forms, no setup, no maintenance. Rambling, imperfect input is the expected input.
SignalCapture-to-first-action latency; share of captures that reach a completed or consciously-dropped item.
M2

The always-visible external brain

Now → maturing
AdvanceMature iOS glanceable surfaces — Live Activities, Dynamic Island, interactive widgets — plus owning the longitudinal behavioral model (not a chatbot's built-in "memory").
MechanismOut-of-sight-out-of-mind is neurological reality; time blindness is the most consistent deficit a tool can visibly counter. Visible beats powerful.
BuildPersistent time-externalization ("22 min on this") on the Lock Screen; one honest next thing as a widget; a glanceable visual day-shape; a queryable behavioral model ("you've rescheduled this four times").
Trap dodgedRead-mostly and forgiving — no streak, no overdue-red. Re-entry shows today, not a backlog of failures.
SignalGlance frequency; do always-visible surfaces cut missed departures vs. push-only?
M3

The right nudge at the right moment

◆ Flagship ~1–2 years
AdvanceGenuinely learned proactive timing — the JITAI framework (Nahum-Shani) fed by newly-cheap context: calendar gaps, location transitions, phone-idle, wearable sleep/HRV as gentle context.
MechanismTurns a passive reminder box (which users forget to open) into an assistant that surfaces the right thing at the right moment — the highest-value unlock for ADHD.
BuildAdopt JITAI's six components — including states of vulnerability and receptivity — over the coaching tick; learn interruptibility per user; surface the if-then plan at its cue; wearables adjust tone, never a clinical claim.
Trap dodgedHabituation and mistiming. Nudge effects decay with dosage — hard frequency guardrails and receptivity-gated silence from day one. A mistimed nudge gets the tool permanently muted.
SignalAck-rate without a rise in mutes; proximal effect measured as a micro-randomized trial would.
M4

It does the thing, not just reminds

~1–2 years
AdvanceReliable scoped agentic execution via structured tool-calling / MCP — a bounded, verifiable action space. Not open-ended computer-use agents.
MechanismThe dreaded multi-step admin is a pure task-initiation wall. Reminding doesn't move it; doing it (with confirmation) removes the wall.
BuildPromote delegation from "drafts" to "does" — book the event, send the drafted email, fill a known form — each previewed and confirmed. Communication translation (decode/soften work email) as a first-class tool.
Trap dodgedOver-reach. Never an open browser agent (~1/3 success). One wrong autonomous send costs the trust the product runs on.
SignalCount of avoided-admin items actually completed through the assistant.
M5

The in-the-moment focus guard

Gated · if unblocked
AdvanceApple fixing the Screen Time API (memory limit, adult authorization, iOS-26 regressions). Outside our control.
MechanismResponse inhibition and wrong-target hyperfocus — a gentle nudge when the user drifts off a declared focus block.
BuildOpt-in, dismissible focus guard. Phone-usage signal first (unlocks, app launches — AUROC ~0.83, on-device, no camera). Any focus score is a noisy nudge, never truth (lab 70–90% → field 55–65%).
Trap dodgedBecoming shaming surveillance. Stays opt-in, self-directed, dismissible, framed as help. Don't build the core loop on an unreliable platform.
SignalFocus-block completion; interventions accepted vs. dismissed-with-annoyance.
M6

Anticipatory · ambient · fully offline

3+ years · watch
AdvanceDigital phenotyping; LLM-upgraded ambient home devices; on-device frontier-grade reasoning. Genuine research frontiers — watch, don't stake milestones on them.
BetsPredict a rough executive-function day before it lands and pre-empt it; hands-free whole-room verbal body-doubling; the entire assistant — including sensitive reasoning — running private and offline.
NoteBody doubling is the most-requested, best-evidenced mechanic in the scan — a virtual/recorded double is buildable well before the ambient-hardware version.
CautionPoor commercial track record (Mindstrong, 2023) and iOS privacy limits starve the signal. Research bets, not commitments.
The non-goals

Three traps to stay out of

Staying out of these is as important as any milestone — each is backed by a cautionary tale in the research.

Regulatory

Stay consumer wellness — not a medical device

Akili's prescription game got FDA clearance and still collapsed (~$34M fire-sale, 2024). Disease-treatment claims mean an unsolved LLM-clearance path. Keep claims in EF-support territory and ship freely.

Platform

Don't build a core loop on Screen Time — yet

FamilyControls is unreliable for a self-directed adult app: a 6 MB memory limit that crashes, adult-auth quirks, unaddressed iOS-26 regressions. High value, shaky foundation, partly outside our control.

Scope

Don't promise open-ended agentic autonomy

Computer-use agents fail ~2 of 3 real tasks. Do scoped, verifiable, API/MCP actions with confirmation — never drive a browser to "book my dentist."

Honest audit

Where the app and the evidence diverge today

The current build measured against the research — verified in code, ranked by severity, each tied to the fix that closes it.

01

The deployment model fights the audience

High

Setup and maintenance are the #1 abandonment cause — yet the tool asks its ADHD user to run a Mac mini, Ollama, n8n and Tailscale off a 341-line env file. Fix: a hosted / one-tap onboarding path, or the "system carries the load" thesis leaks out through the deploy step.

02

Emotion regulation is under-built

High

Emotional dysregulation is a core feature (g≈1.17) with no validated ADHD-adult app — yet it isn't a first-class module; all seven are cognitive-EF. It's handled only indirectly via encouragement/recovery. Fix: the emotion-regulation cross-cutting track.

03

The best-evidenced technique is missing

High

If-then implementation intentions (Gollwitzer d≈0.65; restored inhibition to non-ADHD levels) have no representation in the codebase. The location anchor is cue-shaped, but there's no "if [cue], then [tiny action]" builder. Fix: a first-class if-then primitive; feeds M3.

04

The stats screen reintroduces a shame vector

Moderate

The insights screen surfaces a "current success streak" + success rate — the exact loss-based shape the research ties to the abandonment spiral, and it contradicts the app's own forgiveness ethos. Fix: reframe toward return-after-lapse and trend.

05

Scheduling is time-based, evidence favors cue-based

Moderate

The flagship path fits todos into free time windows and pads clock times — but time-based prospective memory is the weakest channel; event/cue-based is stronger. Fix: more cue-triggering, less clock-triggering (relates to #03).

06

Escalation cadence vs. habituation

Partial · mitigated

Escalation is well-justified for time-critical events and quiet-hours/debounce/channel-learning mitigate well. The residual gap: the engine models channel & quiet hours, not an explicit receptivity/vulnerability state. Fix: exactly what M3 closes.

Already well-aligned — so the audit is calibrated: local-first / privacy · miss-as-data for the system · the encouragement / panic / recovery layer · one-tap App-Intent capture · cue-based location anchors · earn-only kids' star charts. The founding thesis — act at the point of performance — is exactly right.

North star: the field's lesson is that the core risk is engagement, not capability — so the metrics are retention-shaped: durable use past the 2-week novelty window, return-after-lapse, and miss-informed improvement over months. An optional waitlist-controlled study (Inflow's playbook, N=154) would put Prefrontal ahead of every competitor with published outcomes — a credibility asset, not a regulatory obligation.
