Self-care checks

Meal, water, meds, bio-break, and wind-down nudges. Turn the whole thing on or off, and set each check's daily target, the hour it starts (and, for bio breaks, the hour it stops), and how often it re-asks. Logging what you've done today happens on the Dashboard card.

Todos

When automatic breakdown is on, Prefrontal breaks a todo you're avoiding into a tiny first step on its own. It's off by default — the "Break it down" button on each todo always works regardless.

Email hand-off (SMTP)

When you delegate a todo to a human assistant, Prefrontal emails them the prep brief from here. Use your provider's SMTP details — for Gmail, an app password, not your login password. Leave the password blank to keep the one already saved. The password is encrypted at rest and never shown again.
